Best players who have played at Barcelona

Lionel Messi

The best player in the world and the top scorer in the club’s history. He has won 10 leagues, 7 Copa del Rey, 4 Champions League and 6 Ballon d’Or with Barça. He is a genius with the ball, capable of dribbling, assisting and finishing with incomparable magic.

Ronaldinho

The club won its first trophy, the Copa Macaya (Catalan championship), in 1902, and its first Copa del Rey (King’s Cup), Spain’s leading national football cup competition, in 1910. The club also participated in its first European competition, the Pyrenees Cup, in 1910 and won it four times between 1910 and 1913.

Xavi Hernández

The brain of Barça and the Spanish national team that dominated world football for years. His vision of the game, his accuracy in passing and his leadership made him one of the best midfielders in history. He won 8 leagues, 3 Copa del Rey, 4 Champions League and 2 European Championships with Barça .
